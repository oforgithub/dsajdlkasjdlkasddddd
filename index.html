About Carlo A. Rolando

Carlo A. Rolando is an aggressive Bay Area litigator with a well-respected plaintiff's personal injury and general litigation practice. Mr. Rolando's personal injury practice represents individuals and their families in wrongful death and personal injury cases stemming from motor vehicle negligence, product liability, premises liability, and construction accidents. Mr. Rolando provides his clients with exceptional legal representation and attentiveness. Every client is provided with Mr. Rolando's direct number, and knows that each letter, meeting, interrogatory, deposition, and court appearance receives his personal attention, skill, and patience with the goal of achieving the best possible legal result.

Attorney Profile

Attorney Carlo A. Rolando

A Bay Area native, Mr. Rolando graduated magna cum laude from Saint Mary's College of California before moving to Indiana to attend law school at the University of Notre Dame. In law school, Mr. Rolando excelled in written and oral advocacy and was selected by faculty members to represent Notre Dame at the American Bar Association's National Appellate Advocacy Competition.

Following graduation, Mr. Rolando earned recognition as law clerk to the Chief Judge of Michigan's 43rd Judicial Circuit Court. His experience and commitment to excellence have been recognized by various legal associations, including The American Bar Association.

Mr. Rolando has spent years fighting America's largest insurance companies and has obtained millions of dollars for injured persons.
